New Taho Biscoff

Indulge in a next-level taho experience with our limited edition Taho Biscoff from Soy Avenue! This special treat combines the silky smoothness of classic taho with the rich caramel flavor of Biscoff cookie spread and real Biscoff crumble on top.

Classic Arnibal

Experience the nostalgic comfort of our Classic Arnibal Taho a timeless Filipino delicacy reimagined for today’s soy lovers. This creamy, silky smooth taho is made with freshly prepared warm soy pudding, drizzled generously with sweet arnibal and topped with a generous layer of sago pearls for that satisfying chew.

Matcha Taho

Elevate your day with our Soy Refreshing Matcha a smooth and creamy blend of premium Japanese matcha and our signature chilled soy milk, poured over ice for a naturally energizing treat.

Cookies & Cream Iced Taho

Our Cookies and Cream Iced Taho combines the silky smoothness of chilled taho with the rich sweetness of crushed cookies, creamy milk, and chewy sago pearls. Topped with whipped cream and drizzled with chocolate syrup, this decadent treat delivers layers of flavor and texture in every sip.

Strawberry and Pandan Flavored Taho

Taho Avenue’s flavored taho features smooth silken tofu, chewy sago pearls, and your choice of sweet strawberry or fragrant pandan syrup. Available hot or cold perfect for any mood, any time!
